Problem Description:

How do coffee-lovers find their roast?

It can be tough to find your ideal cup of coffee in today’s environment where the range of options is quite limitless. People who drink coffee can sometimes struggle to go through the various options to get something that suits their personal preferences. We suggest creating a web app for coffee preferences to solve this problem. Both coffee enthusiasts and beginners will benefit from this web app as it aims to improve and simplify the process of identifying what they want.

Through the process of creating this web app, we plan to expand on previous web apps that attempted to offer suggestions based on the user’s coffee preferences. Websites like Coffee By The Roast have assisted users in figuring out their tastes in coffee and we’ve been inspired to create something similar. Our coffee preference web app will try to improve upon these existing websites by integrating a bigger dataset of coffee data to create better recommendations tailored to users' needs.

Proposed Application:

Our intended and targeted users for our web application are those above the age of eighteen that enjoy coffee and have regular access to the internet. Those who have an interest in coffee may take particular interest in our web application’s features in assisting our users in potentially finding their new favorite coffee and recommendations personalized per each of our features, specifically a tailored questionnaire, geared towards understanding our users preferences, in order to give them the best coffee recommendations. While a majority of our users will be individuals interested in learning more about their coffee preferences, our web application will also provide any sort of user interested in learning more about coffee in general, the tools and resources to do so, allowing our web application to be a universal platform for all coffee lovers.

Upon entering our web application users will first see our home page that introduces our brand, provides a brief description of what we have to offer, and a link directly to our main feature. Our main feature, with the goal of attracting most of our users, is our questionnaire geared towards understanding our user’s preferences and upon submission, providing our users with a tailored response with new coffee that may be of interest to them based on their flavor preferences and other information they provided to our team within the questionnaire along with different blends our users can create on their own. This will be able to provide our users with the personalized recommendations we know they will love. Another page within our web application will be a menu of coffee items that our users will be able to search or filter through. If users are searching for a specific coffee bean or type, they will be able to type the name into the search bar, which will filter out their desired coffee. From the same page, users will be able to filter out coffees based on the type of roast, the nodes, and more. If a user finds a coffee they love and would like to save it, they will also be able to have the option to heart their favorites, which will then save their favorite items in another window for easy access. If our users would like to learn more about what they can make with coffee beans or a coffee machine, we will also have a discover and learn page that will provide our users with recipes and diagrams on how to make different types of coffees with simple recipes. With these features combined we hope to provide our users with the best types of coffee recommendations, personalized results, a menu of coffee items and easy access to the coffees we know they will love.

Users of the coffee preference app will have access to a comprehensive database of coffee reviews and information sourced from the dataset available at Kaggle This dataset contains reviews and ratings for various types of coffee. And also talks about the information about their origin, flavor, and brewing methods. Users will be able to explore and ilter this data to view coffee preferences and recommendations based on factors such as milk preference, sweetness preference, and caffeine sensitivity. And we will use this external dataset to help analyze the relationship between different coffee and users' preferences.

Using the coffee preference app will help solve the problem of users struggling to find the perfect coffee that matches their preferences and needs. The app will analyze users' input on milk preference, sweet preference, and sensitivity to caffeine and cross-reference this information with the dataset. This will enable it to suggest coffee varieties that align with users' specific tastes, ensuring a more enjoyable coffee experience. Helping customers with difficult choices on coffee. Also, users will be able to explore more coffee options. Lots of people always get the same drink for a long time, our App will expand their coffee horizons and help discover new flavors.
