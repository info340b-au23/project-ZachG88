Welcome to our Coffee Education Page where you can learn about the different types of coffee roasts and even how to make your own drinks!

Types of Roasts

Light

A lighter brown color. Generally with the most caffeine between the three. A light roast will have more acidity and hold more fruit and floral flavors.

Medium

A medium brown color. Medium roasts will have a more balanced flavor and sweetness.

Dark

A darker brown color with an oily surface. Dark roasts are generally more bitter with very low acidity.

Doppio

2 shots of espresso into a 3-4 oz. demitasse cup

Cortado

1 shot of espresso

1 oz. of warm milk

1 cm of foam

Into a 5 oz rocks glass

Ristretto

1 shot of espresso into a 2-4 oz. espresso cup, however use half the amount of water as a regular espresso

Latte

1 shot of espresso

8-10 oz. of steamed milk

1 cm of foam

Into a 6-9 oz. coffee mug

Cappucino

2 oz. of steamed milk

Into a 6-8 oz. cappuccino mug

Macchiatto

Mocha

1-2 oz. of chocolate syrup

Americano
